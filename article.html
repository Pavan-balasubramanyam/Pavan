Virat Kohli Announces Retirement from Test Cricket

Indian cricket legend Virat Kohli has officially announced his retirement from Test cricket, bringing an end to a glorious red-ball career. Kohli, known for his aggressive leadership and unmatched passion, redefined India's approach to Test cricket, especially overseas.

Making his Test debut in 2011, Kohli went on to play over a decade of top-level cricket, scoring centuries across challenging conditions. As captain, he led India to historic victories and transformed the team into one of the strongest Test sides in the world.

While stepping away from the longest format, Kohli continues to inspire millions of fans. His legacy in Test cricket will be remembered for discipline, fitness, and an unbreakable fighting spirit that elevated Indian cricket to new heights.
